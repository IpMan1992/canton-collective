Send Thursday or Wednesday at 10:00 AM (recipient-local)

Best opens → 10 AM & ThuBest conversion → ~4 PM & Wed

Highest open rate lands at 10 AM (40.3%) vs your usual 9:30 AM (36.5%), and Thursday is your strongest weekday for opens (39.0%). Highest intent to convert shows up in the afternoon (~4 PM, 0.22% & $0.29/recipient) and on Wednesday (your best-sampled high-conversion day).

Caveat: ~70% of your sends go at 9:30 AM, so hour effects are tangled with which brand dropped and the season. The afternoon and 10 AM edges look real but thin — worth locking in with a clean A/B (9:30 AM vs 10 AM, and one 4 PM test) before treating as gospel.
